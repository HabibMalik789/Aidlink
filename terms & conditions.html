AidLink Terms and Conditions

1. Definitions

- “User” refers to anyone accessing the platform.
- “Donor” is a user who contributes donations.
- “Recipient” is a user who applies for aid.
- “Admin” refers to authorized personnel managing the system.

2. User Responsibilities

  • Donors: Must provide accurate details. Donations are final once processed.
  • Recipients: Must submit valid documents. Fake requests result in suspension.
  • Admins: Must maintain system integrity and verify applications.

3. Data Protection

All personal data is encrypted and securely stored. No user data is shared without consent. Refer to the Privacy Policy for more details.

4. Use of Artificial Intelligence

AidLink uses AI to verify identities and detect fraud. Flagged applications are reviewed manually to ensure fairness.

5. Prohibited Activities

  • Providing false information or documents
  • Exploiting the system or bypassing verification
  • Harassing users or misusing donations

6. Donations and Refunds

Donations are final and non-refundable unless a technical or fraud-related issue occurs.

7. Termination

We reserve the right to suspend or delete accounts that violate these terms or show suspicious activity.

8. Modifications

Terms and Conditions may be updated at any time. Changes will be shared via the website or email.

9. Contact

For any queries, contact us at support@aidlink.org
