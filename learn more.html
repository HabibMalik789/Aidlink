How AidLink Works

Our Mission

AidLink is designed to bridge the gap between verified aid seekers and responsible donors. Our goal is to eliminate fraud and bring AI-powered transparency into the donation process.

For Recipients

Register and fill out the Aid Application form
Upload CNIC and supporting documents
Let our AI verify your details securely
If approved, your case goes live for donors

For Donors

Browse through AI-verified applications
Filter by urgency, type, or suggested matches
Make a secure donation online
Get impact reports & donor satisfaction updates

AI-Powered Verification

Our AI uses OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to read uploaded CNICs and documents, checks metadata, and calculates fraud risk scores. High-risk applications are flagged for admin review to ensure transparency and fairness.

Security & Transparency

AidLink ensures your donations are traceable, your data is encrypted, and your trust is protected. We log every donation, flag suspicious cases, and allow only verified users to receive help.
